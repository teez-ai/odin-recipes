Carbonara Recipe

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, fatty cured pork, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Spaghetti or linguine
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Add bacon and water to pan until simmer.
  2. Continue simmering until little to no water is left on the pan, then continue to cook the bacon until crispy.
  3. Remove bacon from pan and save the bacon fat for later.
  4. Saute garlic in that same pan until golden brown, then add to a bowl with 1 tablespoon bacon fat, eggs, egg yolk, Parmesan and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti or linguine pasta for 5 minutes or until firm. Once cooked, drain pasta and reserve 1 cup of the pasta water.
  6. Slowly pour the hot cooking water from the pasta into the egg mixture. Then pour over the hot pasta and toss to coat. Add crumbled bacon.
  7. Let pasta rest for a few minutes, tossing frequently until the carbonara sauce thickens. Serve immediately with a sprinkle of parsley.
