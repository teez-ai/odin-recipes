French Toast Recipe

Description

French toast is a dish of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and often milk or cream, then pan-fried.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract
  • Optional: Powdered Sugar
  • Optional: Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Pour half cup of milk, eggs, 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon of cinamoon to a shallow dish/bowl and mix
  2. Get your pan and spray cooking oil or spread butter until covered. Make sure pan is medium high heat
  3. Coat bread in mixture on each side.
  4. Place bread on heated pan and toast until its no longer soggy
  5. Set aside bread on plate and add powdered sugar, maple syrup, etc. Whatever you like and Enjoy!
