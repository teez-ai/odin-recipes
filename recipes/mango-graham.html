Mango Graham Float Recipe

Description

Mango float or mango graham cake is a popular Filipino dessert. This icebox cake is made from layers of graham crackers, whipped cream, condensed milk, and ripe mangoes. This whole assembly is chilled and set before being cut into squares to serve.

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 Mangoes
  • 3 to 4 Cups of Heavy Cream
  • Half a Cup of Sweet Condensed Milk
  • 1 to 2 Packs of Graham Crackers

Steps

  1. Peel skin and thinly slice the mangoes or chop. Set aside
  2. Combine heavy cream and condensed milk and whip until stiff peaks form
  3. Crush graham crackers for toppings
  4. Start layering in an aluminum tray or any container.
  5. Lay graham crackers
  6. Then spread the whipped cream on top of the graham crackers
  7. Then add sliced mango on top
  8. Repeat for 1-2 times or until desired
  9. Add the crushed graham crumbs on top once finished layering
  10. Store in freezer of fridge for 30 minutes or overnight depending on your liking
